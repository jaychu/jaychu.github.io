Portfolio

Bell.ca Shop

https://www.bell.ca

Bell.ca is an C# ASP.NET application utilizing an in-house proprietary CMS. Bell.ca has hundreds of changes on a daily basis made by several different teams across the Bell IT team. My involvement in this application includes converting the entire application from adaptive to responsive, implementing various different features provided by business, and establishing new lines of communication between the front-end and the back-end. Right now, my role is to manage 6 different agile teams to ensure that their deliverables are successful.

Capital B Wines

https://capitalbwines.com/

This beautiful wine catalogue is built on Django hooked up to a PostgreSQL database runs on AWS shows off a bountiful selection of rare wines curated by Capital B Wines. Using Django's powerful admin tools, they are able to modify their data on the fly and clients can reach out to them using a contact us form protected by captcha.

Bell Support

https://support.bell.ca

Bell Support was one of my first projects with Bell. It is built as a C# ASP.NET application utilizing an in-house proprietary CMS. Support has a bunch of automated changes happening on the daily basis as well as thousands of pages worth of content management that needs to be managed. My role on this application involved converting this entire application from adaptive to responsive and integrating helpful widgets to improve the support experience for Bell customers.

Facilitate 2

Defunct

Facilitate 2 was an in-house inventory and report tool that was built by students at McMaster working at the University Technology Services. Running on an LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP), the site was used to keep track of inventory and computer malfunctions within the computer labs so that proper maintenance could be done. The site has since been discontinued as the company phased out the student division.

This website was created with Strapi & NextJS hosted on Github Pages
